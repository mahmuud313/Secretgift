My Saddonti, my love, my everything,

Happy Valentine’s Day, my beautiful girl Saddonti. I don’t even know where to start because no words can truly capture how much you mean to me...

I get lost in your eyes every time I look at you—they hold so much love, so much depth, and they pull me in like nothing else ever could...

I’ll never forget that day at the cinema—how we sat close, whispering, laughing, completely in our own little world...

...I love you, my Saddonti. Today, tomorrow, and for the rest of my life. ❤️

أُقسِم بالذي خلقكِ،فسواكِ،فعدّلكِ،أنّي أعشَقُكِ بطريقة لا يستوعبها عَقلُكِ ❤️

This was written from me to you on Valentine’s Day, Saddonti, because I love you so much! Allah ykhalili eyeki ya noor 3yoone! ❤️
